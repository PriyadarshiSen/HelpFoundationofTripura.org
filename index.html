ABOUT US

Help foundation of Tripura is a welfare organisation located in Dhaleshwar, Agartala, Tripura West, India, Pin: 799007. Our organisation has been working for the upliftment of the backward sections of the society. Through our welfare programs along with our development partners we have been able to support the masses. We champion causes like protection of human rights, eradication of hunger and poverty, support during calamities, encouragement of art and culture and other welfare programs with the help of the financial aid in the form of donations. We extend or gratitude to all are supporters and partners for assisting us in our mission.

Areas of Operation

Help foundation operates in less developed areas of Tripura and West Bengal, with the below poverty line population and the ones that are dependent on agriculture to earn their livelihood. Organisation also works for the welfare of destitute women, children in distress, senior citizens, specially abled children and students.

Our Values

A charity organisation, help foundation holds up to its value of uplifting the down trodden through compassion. We work for the protection of human rights, providing education to the rural masses, eradication of hunger and poverty, support in disaster management and encouragement of art and culture through our programmes. For all are programs we make an effort in the maintenance of transparency. We encourage new ideas and does empower people in making effective decisions. Are organisation pursue equitable partnerships and resort to locally lead solutions with our development partners. Being human beings, we have some essential responsibilities towards our society. We must be honest, helpful, and loyal citizens and assist the ones who need help. Moreover, a good citizen ought to gain knowledge and spread it in his surroundings. Social responsibility is an ethical framework and suggests that an individual has an obligation to act for the benefit of society at large. Social responsibility is a duty every individual has to perform so as to maintain a balance between the economy and the ecosystems. Social responsibility means sustaining the equilibrium between the two. It pertains not only to business organizations but also to everyone whose any action impacts the environment. It is a concept that aims to ensure secure healthcare for the people living in rural areas and eliminate all barriers like distance, financial condition, etc. This responsibility can be passive, by avoiding engaging in socially harmful acts, or active, by performing activities that directly advance social goals. Social responsibility must be intergenerational since the actions of one generation have consequences on those following.
