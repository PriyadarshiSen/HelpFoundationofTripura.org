Steps We have Taken


Hunger Eradication Programme

In the year 2019-2020 help foundation had taken up the humble opportunity to feed the hungry. People struggling through poverty were assisted with prepared meals. It was the relief on the face that was reflected in us. We also distributed water bottles during the summer when people suffer from the heat and dehydration also leads to serious health rights like sunstrokes. Are program participated encountering these problems.

Fight Against COVID19

During the COVID19 outbreak in 2020 help foundation took up the responsibility to play it's role against the pandemic. Shoes number of people had lost their jobs and source of income. Carried out distribution of dry ration, hand washes, masks and sanitizers to the economically weaker section of the society, also held and oven is campaign to educate the masses about the virus and ways of protection.

Blood donation camp

On it's 13th foundation day help foundation organised for a blood donation camp.

3 Days Farmers Training Programme

2020 help foundation organised for a 3 day farmers training program to educate the farmers on adopting organic methods of farming. With the help of self help groups the farmers would trained to adopt scientific and sustainable methods of farming.

Women empowerment and training programme

Help foundation works for the upliftment of women from economically backward sections and women who have faced domestic violence. In the year 2020-21 Help Foundation organised for a training program with vision of enabling them to be self sustaining.

Animal husbandry and goat rearing

In the year 2020-21 Help foundation carried out a program for educating the rural masses about animal husbandry and goat rearing. Program included impartment of information on management skills in rearing and help in poverty alleviation in these groups promoting a model bio tool.
