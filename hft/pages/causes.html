Let's Make A Difference


Hunger & Poverty

Hunger and poverty are unfortunate issues for any society to deal with. Hunger needs to be eradicated for every individual to have their basic needs fulfilled. With the huge disparity in income of economic groups poverty becomes unavoidable. Help foundation urges the more fortunate strata of the society to come forward and donate for the eradication of poverty and hunger

Education & Training

Education and training are extremely essential for efficiency. Education is the way to spread awareness and enhance the standard of living through further training and thus better job generation. It is sad that even in this modern era over 25% of the population is uneducated. Help foundation aims at promoting education and training among the masses.

Human Rights

Human rights are moral principles or norms for certain standards of human behaviour and are regularly protected in municipal and international law. These are the rights that are deemed to be essential for a rightful human existence. Unfortunately there have been wide violation of these rights through unethical activities like war crimes, human trafficking and child labour. 40 million children below the age of 15 suffer from abuse. Help foundation aims to render our contribution for the protection of human rights.

Arts & Culture

Art and culture bring the necessary colours to our lives and are proven to be a key factor in setting and maintaining of values in a society. The promotion of art and culture leads to job generation and eases day to day functioning. It is our aim to promote artists and cultural values for a better society.
